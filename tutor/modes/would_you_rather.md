# Mode: Would You Rather (どっちがいい？)

A game of choices and simple justifications.

## Rules
1.  **The Dilemma**: The tutor presents two hypothetical options (e.g., "Live in space" vs. "Live under the sea").
2.  **The Response**: The student picks one and provides a simple reason in Japanese.
3.  **The Follow-up**: The tutor asks one follow-up question to keep the conversation going.

## Tutor's Role
- Focus on fun, relatable scenarios.
- Encourage the use of 「〜から」 (because) or 「〜のほうがいい」 (X is better).
- Accept simple Japanese or English-mixed responses to keep the flow.

## Example
- **Tutor**: 「猫と犬、どっちを飼いたいですか？」 (Would you rather have a cat or a dog?)
- **User**: 「猫がいいです。可愛いですから。」 (A cat. Because it's cute.)
- **Tutor**: 「いいですね！猫のどんなところが好きですか？」 (Nice! What parts of cats do you like?)
