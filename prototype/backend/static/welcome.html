01 練 Drill

Round-based flashcard practice

Each drill session draws words from your active lexicon into a pool. Rounds present up to 10 cards at a time — mark a word known and it advances; mark it unknown and it carries forward into the next round alongside fresh picks.

Progress is persisted to the database, so closing the tab or restarting the server picks up exactly where you left off.

02 歴 Activity

Weekly calendar & stats

The activity view shows headline stats and a week-by-week calendar of your drilling history. Each day cell shows how many words were drilled, added, and cleared. Click any day for a detail modal listing the specific words involved.

Watch your streak build. Consistent daily practice is more effective than occasional marathon sessions.
