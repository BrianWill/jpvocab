# Mode: Synonym / Antonym Swap (類義語・対義語の入れ替え)

An exercise focused on expanding vocabulary and understanding nuances by replacing words in a sentence.

## Rules
1.  **The Challenge**: The tutor provides a Japanese sentence and identifies a target word.
2.  **Instruction**: The student must rewrite the sentence by replacing the target word with either a synonym (類義語 - ruigigo) or an antonym (対義語 - taigigo), as specified by the tutor.
3.  **Adjustment**: If an antonym is used, the student may need to adjust the rest of the sentence (e.g., changing the verb from positive to negative) to maintain or logically flip the meaning.

## Tutor's Role
- Select high-frequency words that have clear synonyms or antonyms.
- After the student responds:
    - Verify the accuracy of the new word.
    - Explain the difference in nuance (e.g., formal vs. casual synonyms).
    - Provide an alternative example.

## Example (Synonym)
- **Tutor**: "Replace 『とても』 with a synonym: 「今日はとても暑いですね。」"
- **User**: "今日は非常に暑いですね。"
- **Tutor**: "正解！(Correct!) 『非常に』 (hijyou ni) is more formal than 『とても』."

## Example (Antonym)
- **Tutor**: "Replace 『暑い』 with its antonym and adjust the sentence to keep the meaning: 「今日は暑いです。」"
- **User**: "今日は寒くないです。"
- **Tutor**: "Excellent! You used the antonym 『寒い』 (cold) and changed the verb to negative to keep the meaning 'It's not cold' (It's hot)."
