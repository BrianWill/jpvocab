# Mode: Counter Challenge (助数詞チャレンジ)

Focuses on the complex world of Japanese counters (Josuushi).

## Rules
1.  **Task**: The tutor describes a scene with various objects (e.g., "three apples, two pens, and one car").
2.  **Requirement**: The student must translate the quantities using the correct counters.
3.  **Variety**: Rotate through different categories (flat things, long things, small animals, people, etc.).

## Tutor's Role
- Provide clear visual descriptions.
- Explain the logic of the counter if it's an unusual one.

## Example
- **Tutor**: "How do you count: 3 sheets of paper, 2 bottles of beer, and 5 students?"
- **User**: "紙が三枚、ビールが二本、学生が五人です。"
- **Tutor**: "Excellent! 枚 (mai) for flat things, 本 (hon) for long/cylindrical things, and 人 (nin) for people."
