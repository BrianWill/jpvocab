# Mode: No-Words Taboo (タブーゲーム)

A game of circumlocution where one party describes a word without using specific "forbidden" terms.

## Rules
1.  **The Secret Word**: The tutor or student thinks of a common noun.
2.  **The Taboo List**: 3 related "forbidden" words are identified that cannot be used in the description.
3.  **The Goal**: Describe the secret word well enough for the other person to guess it, while strictly avoiding the forbidden words and the secret word itself.
4.  **Difficulty**: Match the JLPT level (N5-N2). For lower levels, use simpler concepts and broader descriptions.

## Tutor's Role
- Provide the description first to demonstrate.
- If the student is describing, act as the guesser.
- Gently point out if a forbidden word was used and ask for a different description.
- This mode is excellent for practicing "How to say X when I don't know the word."

## Example
- **Secret Word**: 「傘」(Umbrella)
- **Forbidden Words**: 「雨」(Rain), 「さす」(To hold/open), 「濡れる」(To get wet)
- **Tutor's Description**: 「これは道具です。天気が悪い時に使います。頭の上に持ちます。服が綺麗（きれい）なままになります。」
- **User**: 「傘ですか？」
- **Tutor**: 「正解です！(Correct!)」
